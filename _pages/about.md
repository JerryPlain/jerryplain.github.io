I am a Master's student in Robotics, Cognition, Intelligence at the [Technical University of Munich](https://www.tum.de/en/). I am currently a research assistant at the [CV4DT Group](https://cv4dt.github.io/) at the University of Cambridge, working with Dr. Zhengyang Ling and [Dr. Guangming Wang](https://guangmingw.github.io/), and at TU Munich's [AIR Lab](https://www.ce.cit.tum.de/en/air/home/) and [CAMP](https://www.cs.cit.tum.de/camp/start/), advised by Prof. Alois Knoll and Prof. Nassir Navab. I also work with [Prof. Volker Tresp](https://tresp-lab.github.io/) at LMU Munich on agentic multimodal evaluation and automated agentic system design. Before this, I was a research intern at the Huawei Munich Research Center, advised by [Dr. Krishna Kanth Nakka](https://krishnakanthnakka.github.io/). I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering and Automation from Changsha University of Science and Technology.

My research lies at the intersection of **robotics**, **embodied AI**, and **foundation models**. My long-term goal is to develop general-purpose robotic agents capable of robust perception, reasoning, and manipulation in open-world environments. I am currently interested in:

- **Multimodal Learning** for robot perception.
- **World Models** for prediction, reasoning, and planning.
- **Foundation Models for Robotics** for scalable and generalizable robot learning.
