- Developed a geometry-aware real-to-sim pipeline that scales sparse robotic ultrasound acquisitions into large-scale synthetic datasets through CBCT reslicing, LC2 registration, paired ultrasound rendering, and physically valid trajectory generation.
- Benchmarked general, medical, and ultrasound foundation models for robotic ultrasound segmentation, evaluating the impact of geometry-aware synthetic data under limited real-data supervision.
