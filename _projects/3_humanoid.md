- Built a ROS2–PyBullet–Pinocchio simulation and control stack for the TALOS humanoid, implementing floating-base dynamics, inverse dynamics control, and whole-body TSID for standing and single-leg balance.
- Integrated ZMP/DCM-based balance strategies with LIP-MPC footstep planning and swing-foot trajectory generation to achieve stable walking in simulation, with disturbance rejection experiments and quantitative evaluations.
